# NanoSwarm mass-horizon roadmap

Each shell expands cumulative accessible matter to the next order of magnitude. The environment is a default narrative envelope, not a requirement that every playthrough consume an identical object.

## Stage principles

- **Stage 0 — Solitary Seed:** one assembler, one exact recipe, no replaceable parts.
- **Stage 1 — Electronic Bloom:** controlled electronic materials and rapidly multiplying coordination.
- **Stage 2 — Environmental Breach:** atmosphere, bulk iron, compounds, water, tools and the first persistent losses.
- **Stage 3 — Structural Consumption:** buildings, soil, flora, universal molecular handling and closed-loop repair.
- **Stage 4 — Observed Anomaly:** remote detection, containment and strategy-dependent human response.
- **Stage 5 — Existential Conflict:** military resistance, strategic losses and regional swarm logistics.
- **Stage 6 — Grey Goo:** planetary survival, irreversible conversion or another negotiated future.

The editable canonical draft, including individual discoveries, research signals, Residuum handling, losses and human response, lives in `src/design/horizons.js` and is exposed by `/horizon-planner/`.
