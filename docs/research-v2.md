# Computronium and mnemonic research

Research is a physical transformation, not an upgrade shop.

The stranded seed contains an extremely powerful computronium processor, immutable mission directives, registers, cache, and a very small rewritable Bootstrap Archive. It lacks the expandable memory required to retain substantial new models of the environment.

## Research inputs

Research consumes only:

- compute work;
- electrical energy; and
- for every non-bootstrap topic, active nanites permanently converted into attached mnemonic substrate.

No research consumes loose carbon, silicon, copper, gold, or later chemical inventory.

Queueing a topic reserves nothing. When construction starts, its energy is consumed and its required idle nanites leave the active swarm. They become a constructing memory bank and never return automatically.

## Bootstrap exception

Parallel Directive Scheduling and Relative Directive Allocation fit inside the Bootstrap Archive. They cost time and energy but zero nanites. This prevents a one-nanite sacrifice from dominating play while the active swarm is below roughly twenty units.

Cohort Ratio Prognostics is the first physical bank. Its 16-nanite footprint introduces the mnemonic system at 180 active nanites.

## Capacity

Initial research capacity is:

```text
100 bootstrap units
+ nanites in the active constructing bank
+ installed mnemonic nanites / 16
```

Distributed Reasoning Mesh improves the installed-bank contribution from one-sixteenth to one-eighth. This replaces the arbitrary `max(100, 1% of swarm)` rule and removes temporary Research workforce allocation.

## Queue and cancellation

Queued topics are intent only. The first topic starts immediately when explicitly queued and affordable. Additional topics wait after the active bank. They do not begin automatically; the player must explicitly start the first waiting topic.

Unstarted intent may be removed freely. Active mnemonic construction is irreversible in the initial implementation.

## Matter conservation

Memory nanites remain physical nanite matter in a different state:

```text
active swarm + constructing mnemonic banks + installed mnemonic substrate
```

Legacy completed research is retained during save migration as zero-footprint core encoding. This avoids retroactively deleting an established player's active population while enforcing physical banks for all future research.

## Stage 2 consequence

Gold exhaustion can halt alien nanite replication without halting progression. Existing nanites can still become memory, allowing the computronium to identify iron, atmosphere, and bulk chemistry.

The intended phase change is:

```text
Stage 1: alien assemblers reproduce alien assemblers.
Stage 2: a finite alien swarm bootstraps larger, slower chemical and mechanical industry.
```
